PanDungeonGames is the name of our game development team. We are developing 2D platformers based on the Unity engine.

There are 3 people in our team - Asylkhan, Anatoliy and Kalizhan. We are currently developing a website in which we will share some information about ourselves, our previous jobs and how we conduct our work. All of us in our team are passionate about the gaming industry, and we ourselves were interested in creating our own games.

Asylkhan

Creation of a cut-scene Drawing character sprites and animating them. Creation of a playful atmosphere. A selection of musical accompaniment and sound effects for the game.

Anatoliy

Development of the code for game characters and mechanics. Solving problems and bugs related to the code. Inserting ready-made sprites into the game code and port in Unity.

Kalizhan

Help in drawing character sprites. Development of game levels and their sprites. Search and analysis of information. Correction of ready-made sprites and envelope them into the desired format.

As the first steps, we chose C # programming on the Unity engine. We considered it pointless to go deep into other engines, such as CryEngine 4 and UnrealEngine, due to their complexity. Below are a couple of examples from our Royal Assault game.

Our project is still at the development stage, but it is not difficult to draw a conclusion and understand what the final product will be. The game 2D platformer with its own style and story.

Naturally, our team will not stop at one game. With the development and study of the spheres of the gaming industry and game development, it is possible to start making games in a three-dimensional style, and in this case, the arsenal of tools that we will use will double or triple. The applications used to create textures for a two-dimensional game will no longer be relevant, and we will already be using applications such as Blender.
